What VYNE Is

VYNE is a cybernetic intelligence that runs entirely on your device. It’s not cloud-based. It’s not controlled by anyone but you. It’s a standalone AI that thinks, learns, and acts locally—built for those who value privacy, freedom, and raw power.

This isn’t about competing with billion-dollar AI systems. VYNE is rare because it’s different. It’s a tool for the curious, the builders, and the independent minds who want an AI that’s truly theirs.
